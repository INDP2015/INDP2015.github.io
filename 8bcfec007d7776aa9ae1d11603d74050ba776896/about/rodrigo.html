Rodrigo Dias

Cortical Circuits Lab

Email: rodrigo.dias@neuro.fchampalimaud.org

Summary:

I've always wanted to know how "things" work. When I learned about the possibility of using the skills I obtained in my Physics Engineering course to study the brain, I started working at the Champalimaud Research center. I am currently doing PhD in Neuroscience in the Cortical Circuits Laboratory. I am also the proud father of a 2 year old and I love to spend time with family and friends.

Origin:

Lisbon

Education:

Physics Engineering (2011 BSc and 2013 MSc - Universidade de Lisboa - Instituto Superior Técnico)

Interests:
